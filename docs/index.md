## Introduction

* **Garrett Wiebke is a member of Team 307 and is contributing to the Submersible Exploration Device by designing and implementing the actuation system, effectively allowing the device to move.**

### Project Summary

* Team 307 is designing a submersible exploration device, designed for measuring environmental data and navigating through unknown aquatic environments. Garrett will be fulfilling the SPI serial communication portion of the project requirements by communicating between a motor driver and an ESP32 chip, allowing the device to move in an aquatic environment. To see the full data sheet with the combined efforts and documentation of all Team 307, please see the link to the team data sheet below.
